Services Provide

Explore our comprehensive range of dental services designed to enhance your oral health and bring out your best smile. From routine check-ups to advanced treatments, we're here to cater to your dental needs with expertise and care. Discover how our services can transform your dental experience today.

Dental Checkup

Dental Check-up is a comprehensive examination of your oral health, including a thorough assessment of your teeth, gums, and overall dental condition. During this routine evaluation, our experienced dental professionals will identify any potential issues, provide recommendations for preventive care, and help you maintain a healthy and radiant smile.

Teeth Cleaning

Teeth Cleaning, or dental prophylaxis, is a meticulous and gentle procedure performed by our skilled dental hygienists. It involves the thorough removal of plaque and tartar from the surfaces of your teeth, leaving them fresh, smooth, and more resistant to decay. This essential service not only enhances your oral hygiene but also contributes to a brighter and healthier smile.


Book Now

Cavity Fillings

Cavity Fillings, also known as dental fillings, are a dental procedure used to repair teeth that have been damaged by tooth decay. During this procedure, the dentist removes the decayed portion of the tooth and fills the space with a suitable dental material, such as composite resin or amalgam, to restore the tooth's strength and function.


Book Now

Tooth Extraction

Tooth Extraction is a dental procedure in which a damaged or problematic tooth is carefully removed from its socket in the jawbone. It is performed to address various dental issues, including severe decay, infection, or crowding, and it can help alleviate pain and prevent further oral health complications.


Book Now

Braces Installation

Braces installation involves attaching orthodontic brackets and wires to the teeth to correct misalignment. The brackets are carefully bonded to the teeth, and wires are threaded through them. Over time, these braces are adjusted to gradually shift the teeth into their desired positions, resulting in a straighter and healthier smile.


Book Now

Teeth Whitening

Teeth whitening is a cosmetic dental procedure aimed at lightening the color of teeth and enhancing their appearance. Typically, it involves the application of a bleaching agent to the teeth or the use of specialized dental equipment, such as laser or LED lights, to accelerate the whitening process.


Book Now

Gum Treatment

Gum treatment, also known as periodontal therapy, involves the diagnosis and management of gum diseases like gingivitis and periodontitis. The procedure typically includes deep cleaning, scaling, and root planing to remove plaque and tartar buildup from below the gumline, helping to improve gum health and prevent further complications.


Book Now

Denture Fitting

Denture fitting is a process in which custom-made artificial teeth (dentures) are created to replace missing natural teeth. It involves taking impressions of the patient's mouth to ensure a precise fit. Once the dentures are fabricated, they are carefully adjusted and positioned in the mouth to restore oral function and appearance.
